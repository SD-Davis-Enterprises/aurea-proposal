SUBSYSTEM — 01
Hydroponic Crop Bays
Vertical stacked growth towers with aeroponic adaptation for Martian gravity (0.38g). Crops include potatoes, wheat, soy, leafy greens, and strawberries. Optimized for caloric density per watt of lighting input.
Aeroponics 0.38g Adapted LED Spectrum
SUBSYSTEM — 02
Protein Bioreactors
Dual algae reactors (Spirulina) producing fast oxygen and complete protein. Fungal bioreactors (mycoprotein) for meat-analog nutrition. Optional insect system fed from crop waste stream. Combined DHA algae strains address omega-3 gaps.
Spirulina Mycoprotein B12 Supplemented
SUBSYSTEM — 03
Autonomous Crop Drone System
Overhead rail-mounted robotic system for plant health monitoring, pollination, disease detection, and harvesting. Eliminates routine agricultural labor. <4 crew-hours per week at full operation.
ACDS Computer Vision Rail-Mounted
SUBSYSTEM — 04
Regolith Nutrient Extraction
Processes Martian soil through a multi-stage pipeline: perchlorate bioremediation via perchlorate-reducing bacteria (Dechloromonas sp.), acid dissolution of phosphates, and atmospheric N₂ capture + nitrogen-fixing bacteria to close the nitrogen loop.
Perchlorate Remediation N-Fixing Bacteria In-Situ
SUBSYSTEM — 05
Water Recovery System
Condensation recovery from atmosphere, urine purification (also primary nitrogen source — ~11g N/L × 22L crew output daily), and microbial filtration. Urine-N explicitly routed into nutrient cycle.
>95% Reuse Urine-N Loop Microbial Filter
SUBSYSTEM — 06
AUREA OS — Control System
AI-driven ecosystem management with predictive crop modeling. Communication latency to Earth (4–24 min) makes autonomous AI control mandatory, not optional. Rad-hardened primary controllers with shielded consumer hardware for non-critical monitoring.
Autonomous AI Rad-Hardened Manual Override
